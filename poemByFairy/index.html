Fairy's Poem

发表于 2021-07-31 更新于 2021-11-28

Rose’s inadvertent tenderness

The most sharp decorate the world’s cuteness

I saw the sky full of stars decorating your eyes that night

And your eyes adorn the other side of me

I am lost in summer chasing wind and snow, the breeze blows down the leaves

But everything is just my daydream

I’m abandoned by the world, but you’re the only one who never left

Take me to see the sea and seagulls before I lose my sight

Listen to the rainbow and white clouds before being deaf

Before losing you, it made me feel really lingering

Loyalty and changes

The lone geese holding hands with the hazy sunset

Resolutely hold the indecisive

The broken mirror that meets again is reunited

Will it just for the more thorough breakness

Can’t wait, too late, I’m sorry

Please forgive me for leaving without saying goodbye

Although much time had passed

I love you still

By Sally
